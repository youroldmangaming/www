The Duality of Art & Science

Yin and Yang, with deep roots in Chinese philosophy, represent the duality and interconnectedness of seemingly opposite forces. Like art and science, they are complementary forces that together create a dynamic system where the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.

Art and science may seem like opposites, but they have influenced each other for centuries. During the Renaissance, both fields relied on precise visual observation, with figures like Brunelleschi and Leonardo da Vinci playing pivotal roles.
